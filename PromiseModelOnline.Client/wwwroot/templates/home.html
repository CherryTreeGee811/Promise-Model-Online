Product overview

Built for the Promise Model workflow

The app is designed to support the documented SRS flows: create the stack, collaborate on items, manage strides, and share progress through a single browser-based interface.

Stack-first organization

Create and navigate the full Promise Stack with enforced parent-child relationships across each level.

Shared project work

Invite teammates, assign permissions, and keep comments and mentions visible to the right people.

Stride planning

Move Moments in and out of strides, track backlog items, and keep effort estimates visible.

Portable delivery

Use the application through the SPA and API, with Docker-based deployment and JSON data interchange.

Promise Stack

The five levels of the model

Each layer provides a clearer refinement of intent and execution, which is why the hierarchy is the core navigation structure in the app.

01

Product Promise

The highest-level outcome the project is trying to achieve.

02

Epic

A major body of work that breaks the promise into coherent themes.

03

Journey

A path of value delivery that connects the Epic to implementable work.

04

Flow

A sequence of related steps that forms a usable slice of the work.

05

Moment

The smallest actionable item, classified as a Story or Job for day-to-day execution.
